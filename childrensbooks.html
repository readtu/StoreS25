Children's Books

  • Charotte's Web
    • Charlotte's Web is a story of friendship, courage, and self-sacrifice. Wilbur is a pig who finds out that he is destined for slaughter. His friend, a spider named Charlotte who lives in the doorway of his pigpen, determines to save him; she accomplishes this by spinning words about him in her web.
    • Price: $10
  • Corduroy
    • Corduroy is a small stuffed bear who lives in a department store and waits to be taken home by somebody special. One day a little girl named Lisa wants to buy him, but her mother says no because he is missing a button on his corduroy overalls. That night Corduroy goes in search of his missing button.
    • Price: $9
  • A Wrinkle in Time
    • "A Wrinkle in Time" tells the story of Meg Murry, a young girl who embarks on a cosmic journey with her brother Charles Wallace and friend Calvin to rescue their missing scientist father, who has been captured by a malevolent force called "IT" on a dark planet called Camazotz; guided by three mysterious women, Mrs. Whatsit, Mrs. Who, and Mrs. Which, they must navigate through time and space to confront IT and use the power of love to overcome its evil influence.
    • Price: $14
  • Frog and Toad
    • Frog and Toad are Friends tells the story of two friends of different personalities and the adventures they get into. Each story explains how the friends view the situations they find themselves in and uses humor to help children understand how to accept the different personalities of others.
    • Price: $8
