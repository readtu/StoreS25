Pokémon SoulSilver is a role-playing video game developed by Game Freak and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo DS. Released in 2009 as a remake of the original Pokémon Silver, it revitalizes the classic adventure with enhanced graphics, updated gameplay mechanics, and new features. Players embark on a journey through the Johto and Kanto regions, capturing and training Pokémon to become the Pokémon Champion. A notable addition is the ability for any Pokémon in the player's party to follow them in the overworld, adding a personal touch to the experience. The game also includes the Pokéwalker, a pedometer accessory that allows players to earn in-game rewards by walking in the real world.
